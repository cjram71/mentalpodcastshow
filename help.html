Supporting someone else

Take concerns about immediate safety seriously. Stay with the person when it is safe to do so, contact local emergency or crisis services, and do not rely on a podcast or website as the only source of help.

Medical and therapeutic care

For diagnosis, treatment or ongoing mental-health support, contact a licensed healthcare professional or a local health service.
