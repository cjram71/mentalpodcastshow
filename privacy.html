Privacy

Designed to work without collecting sensitive health data.

The launch version stores no user accounts, diagnoses, listening histories or saved mental-health profiles.

Search and filters

Searches and filter selections run locally in the browser and are not stored by this static website.

Submission form

The submission form prepares an email in your own email application. The website does not transmit or store the form itself.

External links

Official podcast sites, listening platforms and helpline services have their own privacy policies.

Future analytics

If analytics are added, this page should be updated before deployment. Privacy-conscious, cookieless analytics are recommended.
