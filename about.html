About the project

Whatever is on your mind, someone is talking about it.

Mental Podcast Show is being built as a curated guide to podcasts about mental health, relationships, emotional wellbeing and the experiences that shape us.

What makes it different

We organise shows around listener needs, formats and points of view—not only charts or popularity. Every listing includes an independent summary, clear editorial labels and a review date.

What it is not

This is not a therapy service, diagnostic tool, emergency service or substitute for medical care. It is a discovery and reference website.

Current stage

The first release contains a small launch collection. The architecture is designed to expand through RSS feeds, direct submissions and future Podcast Index integration.
