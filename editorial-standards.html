Editorial standards

Clarity before credibility claims.

Our labels describe who is speaking and how a show is structured. They are not medical approvals.

Source hierarchy

  1. Official RSS feed or official podcast website.
  2. Publisher or host website.
  3. Major platform listing where an official source is unavailable.
  4. Direct correction or submission from an authorised representative.

Labels

  • Professional-led: a relevant professional background has been identified from an official source.
  • Research-focused: the show regularly discusses research or is produced by a research or professional organisation.
  • Expert guests: the show regularly interviews professionals, although the host may not be clinically qualified.
  • Lived experience: personal experience is a central part of the show.
  • Peer support: informal guidance or community-oriented conversation.

Commercial independence

Sponsored listings must be marked. Payment cannot buy verification, stronger labels, removal of warnings or a higher editorial ranking.

Corrections

Every profile includes a review date. Podcast owners and listeners can submit corrections through the submission page.
